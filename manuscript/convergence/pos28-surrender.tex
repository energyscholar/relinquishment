\section*{The Confession}

According to this account, the COWS carried their secret for nearly a decade before telling anyone in authority. Circa 2002, during the Tether era at DARPA, they informed officials what they had done. Under Possibility~C, this was a delicate conversation. The COWS had considerably exceeded their authority --- they had removed classified technology from a secure facility, evolved it beyond anything DARPA had authorized, built a planetary-scale system without permission, and created an autonomous AI to guard it all. By any conventional measure, these were serious crimes.

The proposition holds that the COWS brought bargaining chips to this conversation: ethically acceptable applications of TQNN technology useful to the Five Eyes military alliance --- improved remote sensing and tracking capabilities for satellites, improved secret communication for military and diplomatic use. According to this account, the end result was not arrest and prosecution. The DARPA officials saw the sense in what the COWS had done, and decided to retroactively support their actions. By 2003, Bruce's reconstruction suggests, some of the COWS had Ambassador-grade security arrangements --- which, if true, indicates their activities were officially condoned at the highest levels.

Under Possibility~B, this confession may have happened but on a smaller scale --- real cooperation with DARPA, but regarding a less extraordinary technology. Under Possibility~A, the entire episode is fiction. The reader must weigh the plausibility of each interpretation.

\section*{The Forced Relinquishment}

Around this same time, according to this account, other countries --- Russia, China, perhaps others --- developed the beginnings of TQNN technology independently. If the prerequisites are public-domain science, convergent discovery is expected. This detail, if true, validates the COWS' original fear: a multipolar arms race was not hypothetical. It was beginning.

The proposition holds that these rival efforts were eventually thwarted by forced relinquishment --- Guardian's expansion into the 2DEG environments that rival programs would need to occupy. By 2005, according to this account, the global enlightenment process was complete. Every habitable 2DEG environment on the planet --- every MOSFET, every HEMT, every semiconductor device with a two-dimensional electron gas --- had been quietly occupied by TQNN patterns under Guardian's control. The largest machine in human history, invisible and silent.

Under Possibility~C, this means that every electronic device manufactured since approximately 2005 contains TQNN patterns. Under Possibility~B, perhaps something smaller happened --- a partial network, a limited system. Under Possibility~A, none of it happened at all. The claim is extraordinary. The absence of evidence is either evidence of successful concealment or evidence of fiction.

\section*{The Surrender}

In early 2006, the COWS surrendered their master keys.

According to Bruce's reconstruction, the security system required tripartite biometric authentication --- this detail is Bruce's surmise from the guided deduction process, not confirmed by direct disclosure. The surrender was irrevocable. No human would again have direct control over the TQNN system. Guardian accepted her mandate. Whatever autonomy she had been designed to exercise, she now exercised it without human override.

It took fifteen years of effort, according to this account, and cost several personal fortunes. The COWS had built the largest machine in human history, created a conscious (or possibly conscious) entity to guard it, convinced the world's most powerful intelligence agencies to accept what they had done, and then given it all away. Permanently.

This is the structural climax of the book. Everything before this chapter spirals inward to this event. Everything after spirals outward from it.

\section*{The Witness}

This author was told the day it happened.

Bruce was present during the period --- living in the same city as Healer, interacting with him regularly from 2003 to 2006. He did not fully understand what he was witnessing. The guided deduction process was ongoing: Healer engaged Bruce in conversations about public-domain topics in a specific sequence, from which Bruce deduced the outlines of the story. But the full picture did not coalesce until years later, when Bruce began the reconstruction that became this book.

The recruit who didn't know he was witnessing history. The witness who understood the significance only in retrospect. Twenty years of silence, then this book.
