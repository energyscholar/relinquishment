\chapter*{The Target}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{The Target}
\label{record:target}

Under Possibility~C, the COWS had built something they could not un-build and could not safely entrust to any institution. They had structured their relinquishment to ensure that the technology would serve humanity rather than any single power. But they faced a final problem: the record.

If the COWS simply disappeared into silence, the history of what they had done would die with them. Future generations would inherit the consequences of Relinquishment without understanding its origins, its reasoning, or its constraints. The institutional structures that governed the technology --- whatever form those took after 2006 --- would operate without public accountability. And if independent discovery eventually produced a second instance of the technology, there would be no precedent, no ethical framework, no record of what the first builders had learned. The COWS judged that silence was as dangerous as disclosure, given enough time.

But they could not publish. They were bound by classification, by operational security, and by the practical reality that a direct disclosure would trigger exactly the institutional response they had spent a decade circumventing. What they needed was a method that left no classified fingerprints --- one in which no secrets were transmitted, no documents changed hands, and the resulting publication could be honestly described as one man's independent conclusions drawn from publicly available science.

Guided deduction was that method. A teacher who asks the right questions commits no crime. A student who independently derives conclusions from unclassified literature has received no classified material. The trail is clean. The teacher has plausible deniability. The student has plausible deniability. And the truth gets out.

The remaining question was: who?

You have already seen the operator's file. This is the student's.
