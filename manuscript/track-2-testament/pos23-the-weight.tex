\section*{Isolation and Disbelief}

Everyone around me told me they thought David was a dangerous madman up to some mysterious mischief. I was mostly not able to share what I learned with others, because the information was too strange, complicated, and outside their frame of reference. In truth, he was something of a madman. I can't imagine anyone surviving what he went through without going partially mad. Also, of course, geniuses often seem mad to those who don't understand them. I never lost faith in David's essential good intentions. Although my trust was shaken several times, it was never broken. Several times I suspected he was lying to me. Years later I learned that he had actually been speaking plain truth to me in every case.

\section*{The Divorce and Loss of Children}

More than a year into this process my (soon to be ex) wife filed for divorce and claimed custody of our children. She had access to a lot of money, far more than I had, and seemed willing and eager to spend it all on lawyers. I was not willing to mount a custody fight for my children, for various reasons, some of them damned obvious.

My ex-wife had demonstrated a propensity for using government power as a method to hurt people she was angry at, and she was very angry at me. She publicly accused me of doing various illegal things, all of them false, but each accusation brought about an official investigation by police, child welfare workers, and possibly the IRS\@.

As a result of this messy divorce, and my loss of child custody, I had no way to get my children out of the United States. I tried to convince my ex-wife that it was important that she and the children leave the United States to the safe refuge that was provided, but she would not listen. She was convinced that my talk about a secret project to ``Speak Truth To Power'' was utter nonsense, or that it was some sort of twisted lie to wrest the children from her. If my children stayed in the USA they would be hostages of fortune, a grave security risk that made me unsuitable to lead the project.

\section*{The Parting}

In August 2006 I informed him that I could no longer assist him, and that I was sick of waiting for something to happen. At about the same time David and his friends decided, quite correctly, that I was now a bad security risk.

David told me that, because my children could not be protected, I was now a security risk to the project. He also told me that I had been rejected for membership in the cDc.

\section*{The Three Blockers}

\srcnote{HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md lines 614-651 | staging/raw/pos23-the-weight.md | 2026-02-15 | Three blockers from decades of telling, triple spiral solution}

From decades of trying to tell this story in person, three structural problems always intervene:

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Knowledge prerequisites:} No one knows quantum physics, topological neural networks, SAS operations, and tradecraft. The story requires background in all four.
\item \textbf{Time:} The telling invariably stretches to many hours. People lose patience.
\item \textbf{Weirdness:} The Aurasys concept is too strange for most people to process.
\end{enumerate}

The solution: a triple spiral structure, three stories approaching the center from different directions, each teaching one domain through narrative. Gradually build the reader's knowledge. Converge at the center where the reader has all prerequisites. The structural model is \textit{Watership Down} --- Richard Adams made readers fluent in Lapine through story.

\section*{The Weight of Knowing}

\srcnote{Autobiography + Speculative Fiction | staging/raw/pos23-the-weight.md | 2026-02-15 | Weight of knowing, impossible to share, cognitive dissonance, life's work}

David's main mathematical, scientific, and technical project will, eventually, be awarded multiple Nobel prizes, one of which (Physics 1998) has already occurred. This project involved the invention of a new general technology, something that has only happened a few times (e.g.\ fire, metallurgy, steam engine, electricity, radio, computer) in human history.

I have no idea where David is now, or even whether he is still alive. I guess either that he is dead, or that he has finally returned home, where he will soon die. Last I saw him, years ago, he was horribly sick, skeletally thin, and seemingly near death's door. I shall never forget him, though, for turning me into an accidental conspirator.

There is an old saying about secrets: ``Three men can keep a secret, but only if two of them are dead''. Some secrets may keep for a few decades, but most eventually leak out. This story revolves around a secret that has been well kept since the mid 1990s. Many people now alive on planet Earth must know something of it, but nearly all seem to be constrained by some sort of Non Disclosure Agreement (NDA), possibly backed by Government secrecy laws.

Very few people study the Physics of the Fractional Quantum Hall Effect. Very few people study Complex System Biology. Few people study Quantum Computation. Few people study NKS, or even know what the acronym stands for. Relatively few people study Artificial and Biological Neural Networks.

The intersection of people who know much about all these scientific disciplines must be quite small. Few people know much about even one of the above topics, much less all five. Each has its own specialized concepts and vocabulary. This particular combination of scientific skills is relevant because they all inter-relate in an unexpected way.
