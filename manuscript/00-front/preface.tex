\chapter*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}

This book tells a story I pieced together from three years of conversations with a man who never told me anything classified. He talked about open-source science — published papers, public lectures, Nobel prizes. I made the connections myself. I have spent twenty years trying to determine whether those connections are real.

I cannot tell you whether the connections I drew are real. The story they imply may be fabrication, exaggeration, or entirely true. I have maintained cognitive dissonance about these three possibilities for more than twenty years. It is now easy for me. This book transmits that dissonance to you, along with every piece of evidence I have, and the tools to resolve it yourself.
