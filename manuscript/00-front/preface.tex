\chapter*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}

This book tells a story I pieced together from three years of conversations with a man who never told me anything classified. He talked about open-source science --- published papers, public lectures, Nobel prizes. I made the connections myself. I have spent twenty years trying to determine whether those connections are real --- whether the story they imply is fabrication, exaggeration, or entirely true. This book transmits that dissonance to you, along with every piece of evidence I have, and the tools to resolve it yourself.

\textit{I would tell this story anonymously and for nothing if I could, but that is not possible. The story requires the witness.}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\hfill\textit{--- Bruce Stephenson, February 2026}
