\vspace{1cm}

This book is structured as a four-pass pedagogical spiral. Each pass tells the same story at increasing depth. You can stop at any checkpoint and have a complete experience at that resolution.

\begin{description}
\item[Linear path (recommended):] Read straight through. The chapters are arranged so that every concept is introduced before it is needed. This is the intended experience.

\item[Chronological timeline:] Use the timeline appendix to read events in chronological order.

\item[Graphics toggle:] In a PDF viewer that supports Optional Content Groups, you can toggle the ``Graphics'' layer on or off.
\end{description}

\vspace{1cm}

The margin stripes indicate which narrative thread you are reading:

\begin{itemize}
\item \textcolor{trackone}{\rule{12pt}{12pt}} \textbf{Track 1: The Scientist's Confession} --- the inside story
\item \textcolor{tracktwo}{\rule{12pt}{12pt}} \textbf{Track 2: The Recruit's Testament} --- Bruce's first-person account
\item \textcolor{trackthree}{\rule{12pt}{12pt}} \textbf{Track 3: The Machine's Awakening} --- Guardian's arc
\item \textcolor{trackconv}{\rule{12pt}{12pt}} \textbf{Convergence} --- where all three tracks meet
\item Chapters without a margin stripe are \textbf{Bridge} chapters --- historical or scientific context that connects the tracks.
\end{itemize}
