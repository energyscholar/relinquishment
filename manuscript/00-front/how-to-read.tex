This book is structured as a triple spiral --- three narrative threads that interleave and converge. You can read it in several ways:

\begin{description}
\item[Linear path:] Read straight through. The chapters are arranged in a spiral interleave that builds all three threads simultaneously. This is the intended experience.

\item[Per-track standalone:] Follow one track at a time. Each track (Confession, Testament, Awakening) can be read independently as a self-contained narrative.

\item[Chronological timeline:] Use the timeline appendix to read events in chronological order across all tracks.

\item[Graphics toggle:] In a PDF viewer that supports Optional Content Groups, you can toggle the ``Graphics'' layer on or off to show or hide all figures and diagrams.
\end{description}

\vspace{1cm}

The margin stripes indicate which track you are reading:

\end{itemize}
