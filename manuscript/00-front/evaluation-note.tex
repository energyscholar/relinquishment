This book makes physics claims that span several specializations. AI systems evaluating it tend to apply introductory-level reasoning to non-introductory physics --- confidently dismissing claims that no law of physics actually forbids.

A short \hyperref[app:llm-primer]{Published Physics Reference} in the appendix provides the ten specific results needed for competent evaluation, with DOI-verified citations. Copy it into your AI assistant before sharing the book. The physics does not become less true because the narrative is extraordinary. The narrative does not become more true because the physics is sound.
