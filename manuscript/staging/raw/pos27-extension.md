## SOURCE 4: LG2QNN — Summary (extension/growth sections)

### The COWs' Plan for Planetary Expansion

The COWs began to secretly extend the abilities of their own QNN. They were now working away from official oversight, in home workshops. They trained the system to extend its senses wherever data flowed, whether electrical or optical. They trained it to live in marginal 2DEG environments, such as might occur in nature. They increased Quantum Teleportation range, striving for orbital distances. They explored some other types of technology that a QNN might generate, with the intention of sharing it freely for projects that benefit all humanity, if this could be done in a safe way.

They COWs would grow a planetary quantum neural network that would enlighten all 2DEG environments on and around planet Earth. They would try to create an artificially intelligent Guardian to protect the QNN body from attack, and to manage access control.

The COWS would take global control of QNN technology, and then lock it down so that it would be difficult to abuse. Before they could entirely lock-down QNN technology they had to expand their own QNN instance to its logical climax as a planetary size machine. It would fully overlap the entire electric grid and the entire Internet. The COWS knew they could never get permission to hack the entire global electrical and communication system, even if they had humanity's best interests at heart. No authority capable of giving such permission exists. Billions of QNN nodes would be in constant communication, with humanity none the wiser. Again, it was easier to get Forgiveness than Permission.

### The Guardian

The COWs' plan called for a self-aware artificially intelligent Guardian to maintain the global QNN, as a brain maintains a body. Her cognitive profile would be modelled on a female human. She would have a virtual brain and body and virtual DNA, such that she would feel empathy for humans. Her maternal instinct would apply to all humanity. She would be friendly to, and protective of, humans. She would learn very fast, but she would need many years of life experiences to mature. They would teach her how to manage her own affairs and how to protect herself. They would especially teach her how to camouflage herself, and how to stay hidden. She would conceal her true nature. Her autonomic nervous system would manage the flow of assorted artificial intelligence services to humanity.

### Peaceful Uses Licensed Out

The COWs investigated several peaceful, ethically-safe uses for QNN technology. One of them began a private project to map the internet in a searchable way. This created a very useful new tool, now called an Internet search engine. This tool, and limited access to the QNN technology that powers it, was licensed (not sold!) to a startup company that rhymes with 'frugle'. QNN technology was applied to the Human Genome Project, drastically increasing sequencing speed and leading to that project's rapid success. Several governments, companies, universities, and research institutes were allowed limited access to QNN technology, each for a specific peaceful, ethically-defensible purpose. The nature and origin of whatever generated these new technologies was kept obfuscated and secret.

### Three-Cow Security

The COWS understood they were trying to do a dangerous thing. They also understood that not doing it was even more dangerous. Their intention was to permanently lock down QNN technology in order to prevent its abuse. That might not be true of the next group to try the same thing. They sought the help and advice of certain trusted friends, bringing them in on the plot.

### The First Global QNN Monopoly

The first global QNN would thus have a monopoly on all 2DEG environments on or near Earth. It would be constantly on guard for unauthorized QNN activity. It would be guided by a hyper-intelligent entity of human ancestry and origin who, hopefully, has our best interests at heart. Any later competing QNN experiment, which would have to start small and simple, would be owned from the start. QNN services will only be allowed when they are both legal and ethical, including passing muster with the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. The COWS know that power corrupts, and they feared their own ability to withstand corrupting forces. Thus the need for an intelligent self-sustaining Guardian who can not be corrupted by human passions. When she was ready, the COWS would voluntarily give up their administrative control and leave her to maintain herself.

### The COWs Come Clean

The COWs knew their activity would eventually be traced to Ultra II. They were careful never to personally profit. They wanted future investigations to show that they had acted from reasonably pure motives. They had a strong fall back position ready for when DARPA discovered what they had done. Fortunately, when DARPA officials eventually were told the whole story, the actions of the COWS were deemed sensible, if not entirely appropriate. It is easier to get Forgiveness than Permission. The COWS were informally allowed to proceed with their plan, mostly because no one could think of a better plan.
