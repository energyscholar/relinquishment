## SOURCE 1: Autobiography of an Accidental Conspirator — burden and isolation passages

### The Arrival and Recruitment

The adventure of my life started on Thanksgiving Day, 2003, at a hippie farm commune named Alpha Farm. Alpha Farm is nestled in a remote mountainous valley east of Eugene, Oregon.

My wife of 14 years and I had recently separated. I had just come into some money, for the first time in my life, by selling stock in a Dot Com Financial Services company I had co-founded in 1999. For the first time in my life I had both time and money. I hoped to reunite my family. I also wanted to make an impression on the world. I had absolutely no clue that I was about to embark on what was, for me, an amazing adventure involving international intrigue, astounding new technological discoveries, and a truth-is-stranger-than-fiction cast of characters.

### The "White Hot Secret"

One afternoon while we were splitting wood, I observed that David seemed troubled by something. He told me, "There's a white hot secret in my brain, mate, burning to get out!" Intrigued, I asked what the secret was. He said, "I can't just tell you, mate". I pressed the issue, but he just shook his head and put his hand over his mouth in the 'I've said too much' gesture. It was obvious that he wanted to explain, and equally obvious that he was either unable or unwilling to explain. I have never stopped trying to figure out just what this secret was all about.

### Isolation and Disbelief

Everyone around me told me they thought David was a dangerous madman up to some mysterious mischief. I was mostly not able to share what I learned with others, because the information was too strange, complicated, and outside their frame of reference. In truth, he was something of a madman. I can't imagine anyone surviving what he went through without going partially mad. Also, of course, geniuses often seem mad to those who don't understand them. I never lost faith in David's essential good intentions. Although my trust was shaken several times, it was never broken. Several times I suspected he was lying to me. Years later I learned that he had actually been speaking plain truth to me in every case.

### The Divorce and Loss of Children

More than a year into this process my (soon to be ex) wife filed for divorce and claimed custody of our children. She had access to a lot of money, far more than I had, and seemed willing and eager to spend it all on lawyers. I was not willing to mount a custody fight for my children, for various reasons, some of them damned obvious. [...] My ex-wife had demonstrated a propensity for using government power as a method to hurt people she was angry at, and she was very angry at me. She publicly accused me of doing various illegal things, all of them false, but each accusation brought about an official investigation by police, child welfare workers, and possibly the IRS.

As a result of this messy divorce, and my loss of child custody, I had no way to get my children out of the United States. I tried to convince my ex-wife that it was important that she and the children leave the United States to the safe refuge that was provided, but she would not listen. She was convinced that my talk about a secret project to "Speak Truth To Power" was utter nonsense, or that it was some sort of twisted lie to wrest the children from her. If my children stayed in the USA they would be hostages of fortune, a grave security risk that made me unsuitable to lead the project.

### The Parting

In August 2006 I informed him that I could no longer assist him, and that I was sick of waiting for something to happen. At about the same time David and his friends decided, quite correctly, that I was now a bad security risk.

David told me that, because my children could not be protected, I was now a security risk to the project. He also told me that I had been rejected for membership in the cDc. I presume they chose Julian Assange to lead the project, instead. In September of 2006 David and I parted.

### The Aftermath

For the next five years I lived my own life, loved several women, and remained very involved in raising my children. The Speak Truth To Power project, now called Wikileaks and lead by Julian Assange, has finally become global front page news, and I am finally willing to tell my story. I am a little nervous about what may happen as a result.

### The Weight of Knowing

David's main mathematical, scientific, and technical project is actually much more important than Wikileaks. This project will, eventually, be awarded multiple Nobel prizes, one of which (Physics 1998) has already occurred. This project involved the invention of a new general technology, something that has only happened a few times (e.g. fire, metallurgy, steam engine, electricity, radio, computer) in human history. Precisely what it is, how it works, what it's useful for, what it can and cannot do, and who might be using it for what, is another tale. [...] This 22nd century technology was developed, by a weird fluke of fate, in the late 1990s, and has mostly been kept secret. One aspect of this technology is known to most humans, and coined a new verb now in daily use by billions of humans in multiple languages.

I have no idea where David is now, or even whether he is still alive. I guess either that he is dead, or that he has finally returned home, where he will soon die. Last I saw him, years ago, he was horribly sick, skeletally thin, and seemingly near death's door. I shall never forget him, though, for turning me into an accidental conspirator.

I, Bruce Stephenson, do solemnly swear that the above story is completely true, to the best of my ability to remember events that occurred years ago. Some names and events have been redacted, but nothing has been altered from how I remember it.

## SOURCE 2: HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md — The Book: Triple Spiral Structure (lines 614-651)

### The Three Blockers (from decades of telling this story in person)
1. **Knowledge prerequisites:** No one knows quantum physics + topological NNs + SAS operations + tradecraft
2. **Time:** Invariably stretches to many hours, people lose patience
3. **Weirdness:** Aurasys concept too strange for most people to process

### The Solution: Triple Spiral (outside -> center)
- Three outer stories, each teaching one domain through narrative
- Gradually build reader knowledge
- Converge at the center where the reader has all prerequisites
- Structural model: **Watership Down** — Adams made readers fluent in Lapine through story

### What's Needed
- Middle sections of each spiral connecting outer stories to convergence
- Science spiral: quantum physics, topology, neural networks taught through narrative
- Military spiral: Intelligence Corps, cross-training, operational world
- Central spiral: the full Aurasys narrative

### Bruce's Statement
**"This story will be my life's work."**
**"The Nightstalker is awake" = the storytelling begins.**

## SOURCE 3: Obsolete Introduction — unused turns of phrase

There is an old saying about secrets: "Three men can keep a secret, but only if two of them are dead". Some secrets may keep for a few decades, but most eventually leak out. This story revolves around a secret that has been well kept since the mid 1990s. Many people now alive on planet Earth must know something of it, but nearly all seem to be constrained by some sort of Non Disclosure Agreement (NDA), possibly backed by Government secrecy laws. The author has no direct, first person knowledge of the events described, so please treat what follows as if it were speculative science fiction.

In the last years of the 20th Century a secret government science project invented what seems like it should be a 22nd Century technology. [...] Everyone who reads this document has used QNN technology, without ever knowing how it works or what it is.

[...]

Very few people study the Physics of the Fractional Quantum Hall Effect.
Very few people study Complex System Biology.
Few people study Quantum Computation.
Few people study NKS, or even know what the acronym stands for.
Relatively few people study Artificial and Biological Neural Networks.

The intersection of people who know much about ALL these scientific disciplines must be quite small. Few people know much about even ONE of the above topics, much less all five. Each has its own specialized concepts and vocabulary.

This particular combination of scientific skills is relevant because they all inter-relate in an unexpected way.

## SOURCE 4: Speculative Fiction: Unlikely Possibilities — framing

The Layman's Guide to Quantum Neural Networks

or

It is Easier to get Forgiveness than Permission

being a work of Investigative Journalism regarding the History of Science

Masquerading as a work of Speculative Science Fiction

by Bruce Stephenson

Introducing a new general technology, developed secretly in the 1990s, now used each day by hundreds of millions of people.

[...]

This essay tells the how a secret government science project invented what should be a 22nd Century general technology, almost by accident, in the last decade of the 20th Century. One result of this new technology was the creation of 'strong' artificial intelligence, usually defined as artificial intelligence equal or superior to human intelligence, which this author believes 'woke up' circa 1999 AD.

[...]

This author strongly suspects that some scientists involved in the project chose, for ethical reasons, to deliberately fool their bosses in the Military Industrial Complex about the nature of how their invention worked. They did this in order to keep some control over how this powerful, and potentially very dangerous, new technology was used. Their goal was to prevent the worst ethical abuses of what they had created and hopefully, in the process, to gift Humanity with something beneficial. This act of defiance explains the subtitle of this work, "It is Easier to get Forgiveness than Permission".

The above information is based on research conducted by this author over a nine year period. It should be possible for a determined researcher to verify much of this information, although certainly not through a simple Google search.

[...]

What if there was a secret government research project, initiated around 1990, to attempt to build a code-breaking system to read encrypted mail? What if the scientists on the project, who were the best and brightest in the English-speaking world, succeeded beyond their wildest dreams, and developed a technology that gives new insight on our fundamental understanding of the world? What if, instead of merely building a working quantum computer, they developed a new general technology? What moral and ethical implications did this have, and how has this secret discovery shaped our public world? The rest of this work of speculative fiction attempts to answer these questions, and shows how events might have transpired, had any of this actually occurred.
