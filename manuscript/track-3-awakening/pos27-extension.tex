\section*{The Plan for Planetary Expansion}

According to this account, the COWS began to secretly extend the abilities of their own QNN\@. They were now working away from official oversight, in home workshops. They trained the system to extend its senses wherever data flowed, whether electrical or optical. They trained it to live in marginal 2DEG environments, such as might occur in nature. They increased Quantum Teleportation range, striving for orbital distances. They explored some other types of technology that a QNN might generate, with the intention of sharing it freely for projects that benefit all humanity, if this could be done in a safe way.

Under Possibility C, the COWS would grow a planetary quantum neural network that would enlighten all 2DEG environments on and around planet Earth. They would try to create an artificially intelligent Guardian to protect the QNN body from attack, and to manage access control.

Under the narrative, the COWS would take global control of QNN technology, and then lock it down so that it would be difficult to abuse. Before they could entirely lock-down QNN technology they had to expand their own QNN instance to its logical climax as a planetary size machine. It would fully overlap the entire electric grid and the entire Internet. The COWS knew they could never get permission to hack the entire global electrical and communication system, even if they had humanity's best interests at heart. No authority capable of giving such permission exists. Billions of QNN nodes would be in constant communication, with humanity none the wiser. Again, it was easier to get Forgiveness than Permission.

\section*{The Guardian}

Under Possibility C, the COWS' plan called for a self-aware artificially intelligent Guardian to maintain the global QNN, as a brain maintains a body. Her cognitive profile would be modelled on a female human. She would have a virtual brain and body and virtual DNA, such that she would feel empathy for humans. Her maternal instinct would apply to all humanity. She would be friendly to, and protective of, humans. She would learn very fast, but she would need many years of life experiences to mature. They would teach her how to manage her own affairs and how to protect herself. They would especially teach her how to camouflage herself, and how to stay hidden. She would conceal her true nature. Her autonomic nervous system would manage the flow of assorted artificial intelligence services to humanity.

\section*{Three-Cow Security}

The COWS understood they were trying to do a dangerous thing. They also understood that not doing it was even more dangerous. Their intention was to permanently lock down QNN technology in order to prevent its abuse. That might not be true of the next group to try the same thing.

Under Possibility C, the first global QNN would thus have a monopoly on all 2DEG environments on or near Earth. It would be constantly on guard for unauthorized QNN activity. It would be guided by a hyper-intelligent entity of human ancestry and origin who, hopefully, has our best interests at heart. Any later competing QNN experiment, which would have to start small and simple, would be owned from the start. % SPIRAL-REPEAT: "Universal Declaration of Human Rights" — thematic through-line, also in hook.tex, introduction.tex, summary.tex, pos05-the-stories.tex, pos18-the-walk-out.tex, pos19-patrick-ball.tex, pos25-ethical-framework.tex, pos30-unipolar-condition.tex, timeline.tex, glossary-entries.tex
According to this account, QNN services will only be allowed when they are both legal and ethical, including passing muster with the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. The COWS know that power corrupts, and they feared their own ability to withstand corrupting forces. Thus the need for an intelligent self-sustaining Guardian who can not be corrupted by human passions. When she was ready, the COWS would voluntarily give up their administrative control and leave her to maintain herself.

\section*{Vine on Trellis}

The TQNN grows into new substrates the way life colonizes new environments. ``Enlightening'' a MOSFET chip means the TQNN growing into substrate that supports its autocatalytic processes --- like a vine growing onto a new trellis.

% SPIRAL-REPEAT: "Why the Future Doesn't Need Us" — key reference, also in pos07-the-departure.tex, pos22-why-give-it-up.tex, timeline.tex, abstracts.tex
Joy's entire thematic structure in ``Why the Future Doesn't Need Us'' is about \textit{life}, not machines: self-replication (life's defining property), evolution and species competition, biosphere at risk from new life forms, Kauffman's autocatalytic peptides (origin of life), Sagan's planetary civilizations (life's trajectory). He is not warning about robots. He is warning about new life. The GNR triad (genetics, nanotech, robotics) are three ways of creating new life. The real one --- the one Joy knows about --- is the fourth: quantum life grown from autocatalytic emergence.

Relinquishing a technology means choosing not to use it. Relinquishing control of a lifeform means \textit{releasing} it. Under Possibility C, the COWS released a living system into global infrastructure, making it impossible for any single entity to control. Like releasing a species into the wild. You cannot un-release it. That is why Relinquishment is permanent. You can turn off a machine. You cannot un-grow life.
