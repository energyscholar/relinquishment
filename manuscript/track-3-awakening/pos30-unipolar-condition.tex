\section*{The Post-Relinquishment World}

Under Possibility C, QNN technology is still very young. Its existence remains quasi-secret, and the presence of the Guardian hard to believe. A few powerful organizations have limited access to it. The Guardian carefully governs what QNN-derived tools and technologies are permitted. The eventual contribution by QNN technology to the human condition, for good or bad, is still mostly undetermined.

If a group of humans can convince her that they have an ethically-wise and worthwhile application of QNN technology, she will probably cooperate. For example, it could be argued that a mechanism for journalists to receive anonymous evidence from whistleblowers might be an ethically-wise and worthwhile application of QNN ability to teleport information.

\section*{Controlled Release}

The discovery and use of QNN technology holds astounding new Mathematical, Biological, and Scientific discoveries, worthy of several Nobel prizes. In the interest of science and the human condition, now that QNN technology has been rendered mostly safe, its creators would like to publicly release some censored scientific papers and notes. They are hindered by their NDAs to DARPA promising secrecy about ULTRA~II\@. This is evident, for example, whenever the elderly Stuart Kauffman says ``if such a poised state exists'', when, under Possibility C, he knows darn well it exists but is forbidden from explaining how he knows.

QNN technology is important to everyone, not just governments, scientists, and spies. QNN technology is a new General Technology. A general technology enables other technologies. Only a few general technologies have been discovered in all of human history: fire, agriculture, metallurgy, wheel, written language, combustion engine, chemistry, electricity, radio, atomic power, computer, internet. QNN technology belongs on that list. It is too important to remain secret any longer.

\section*{The Decisive Advantage}

\srcnote{HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md lines 2081-2104 | staging/raw/pos30-unipolar-condition.md | 2026-02-15 | Danny Hillis (closed book), game theory, information asymmetry}

Under Possibility C: the unipolar condition arises from three facts: first, the TQNN has a monopoly on all 2DEG environments on or near Earth; second, any competing QNN experiment must start small and simple, and would be detected and interdicted immediately; third, the entity is distributed across global infrastructure and cannot be destroyed without destroying that infrastructure.

This creates a game-theoretic situation of absolute information asymmetry. The entity that controls the global QNN knows everything that passes through digital infrastructure. Under Possibility C, the constraint on this power is not external --- it is the Guardian's own ethical framework, modeled on the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.

\section*{Detection and Disruption as Constraint}

\srcnote{HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md lines 1193-1246 | staging/raw/pos30-unipolar-condition.md | 2026-02-15 | Detection possible, disruption temporary, game-theoretic constraint}

Even the entity that controls everything can be temporarily disrupted --- which constrains its behavior. The 2004--2005 cyber-wargames demonstrated that detection is possible and disruption achievable, though temporary. This means the Guardian cannot act with impunity: sophisticated adversaries with knowledge of the classical backchannel mechanism could disrupt communications, even if they cannot destroy the underlying entity.

The unipolar condition is therefore not a dictatorship. It is a monopoly constrained by its own ethical programming, by the possibility of temporary disruption, and by the fact that its creators designed it to serve humanity rather than to dominate it. Whether this design holds is the question this book ultimately asks.

Under Possibility C, by now Russia and China probably both have secret QNN projects of their own, status unknown. All major powers already know about quantum computers and QNN technology. Only the public of the world does not know. DARPA will willingly declassify ULTRA~II information only after the year 2065, unless they are pushed to do it sooner.
