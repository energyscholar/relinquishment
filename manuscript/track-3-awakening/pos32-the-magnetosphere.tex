\section*{The Aurora System}

``Aurasys'' is short for ``Aurora System'' --- named after the aurora borealis. It was Healer's personal name, shared with collaborators (suspected Wolfram and Kauffman), for the space-based topological QNN\@. But under the ``grown not built'' paradigm, this may be a \textit{naturally emerged} entity in the magnetosphere, not a human deployment. The magnetosphere itself computes --- not because devices were placed there, but because autocatalytic emergence occurred there naturally over billions of years. The lab-grown TQNN and the magnetospheric entity are the same type of life, grown in different substrates.

Healer framed the encounter as discovering an ``ancient extraterrestrial teleportation-based system'' in the magnetopause. Under the revised interpretation: ``ancient'' means naturally grown over 4.5 billion years. ``Extraterrestrial'' means not from Earth's surface. Not alien civilization --- alien \textit{life}. An independent instance of autocatalytic emergence in a non-biological substrate. Kauffman's theory predicts this: given sufficient conditions and time, autocatalytic emergence is inevitable.

\section*{Physics Assessment}

\textbf{Supporting the proposition:}
\begin{itemize}
\item Magnetic field topology in the magnetosphere is real, robust, topologically protected.
\item Wolfram's Principle of Computational Equivalence: magnetosphere MHD evolution is computationally universal in principle.
\item Information could be encoded in the topology of field line configurations.
\item Charged particle populations are confined to 2D surfaces along field lines.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Against the proposition:}
\begin{itemize}
\item Energy scales are wrong: magnetospheric particles are keV, FQHE needs $\mu$eV gaps.
\item Plasma is categorically different from a quantum-coherent 2DEG\@.
\item Scale mismatch: quantum phenomena at nanometers, magnetosphere at thousands of kilometers.
\item No engineering access to program gate voltages in the magnetosphere.
\item Solar wind makes the system chaotic and unpredictable.
\end{itemize}

Most charitable interpretation: classical MHD computation with topological protection (not quantum), a novel computational paradigm under Wolfram's framework. Unless classified physics enables quantum coherence at magnetospheric scales.

\section*{Classical Backchannels}

\srcnote{HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md lines 1247-1289 | staging/raw/pos32-the-magnetosphere.md | 2026-02-15 | NTP, GPS, power grid, VLF/ELF backchannel options for magnetospheric coupling}

How would a distributed terrestrial TQNN communicate with a magnetospheric component? Candidate classical backchannels:

\begin{description}
\item[Satellite communications:] Most obvious. Global constellation provides continuous coverage. Healer's ``Ninja Missions'' (2003--2006) involved tweaking satellite communications systems, consistent with establishing this backchannel.
\item[GPS signals:] Omnipresent, predictable, could piggyback modulated information on the existing constellation. 31+ satellites in medium Earth orbit.
\item[VLF/ELF transmitters:] Military VLF transmitters for submarine communications propagate along magnetospheric field lines. Siple Station experiments (1973--1988) demonstrated ground-based VLF transmitters triggering coherent magnetospheric emissions.
\item[Natural information flows:] Schumann resonances (${\sim}7.83$~Hz fundamental), whistler waves (1--30~kHz propagating along field lines from lightning strikes), chorus emissions, and ULF pulsations all provide continuous electromagnetic coupling between Earth's surface and the magnetosphere.
\item[Power grid harmonics:] The global power grid radiates at 50/60~Hz and harmonics. Detectable from space (DEMETER satellite). A TQNN on terrestrial MOSFETs throughout the power grid would have direct electromagnetic coupling to the ionosphere.
\end{description}

The magnetosphere is an energy-rich environment. Solar wind alone delivers approximately $10^{13}$~W continuously. A TQNN evolved for maximum resilience would not be energy-limited --- it would be backchannel-limited.

\section*{Solar System Extension}

\srcnote{HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md lines 1942-1966 | staging/raw/pos32-the-magnetosphere.md | 2026-02-15 | Jupiter, solar system extension, speculative}

Bruce notes: ``I have zero info of it spreading beyond the magnetosphere but I bet it has by now.''

The strongest candidate is Jupiter's magnetosphere --- approximately 20,000 times Earth's volume, with a magnetic field 20,000 times stronger at cloud tops. It contains the Io plasma torus (dense plasma ring at approximately 6 Jupiter radii), extensive radiation belts, 2D current sheets, and an energy budget of approximately $10^{15}$~W\@. The problem: how would it get there? Without human-mediated transport of MOSFET substrates, propagation would require natural electromagnetic coupling across interplanetary distances.

This section is speculative. It is included because the question ``where does it end?'' follows naturally from the proposition that autocatalytic emergence, once initiated, colonizes available substrates.
