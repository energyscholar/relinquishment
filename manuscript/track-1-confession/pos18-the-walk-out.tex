This story might be based on true events. Then again, it might not.

Extinction is forever. For the one actual extinction event in a species' history there may be many near misses. This is the story of one recent near miss for the human species. This existential crisis has passed, hardly anyone noticed, and we are still here. We may even be better off for it. May these historical and/or fictitious events inspire our species to find wisdom to survive whatever existential threats remain in our collective future.

\vspace{1cm}\noindent\rule{0.5\textwidth}{0.5pt}\vspace{0.5cm}

According to this account, in 1994 a faction of the Ultra II scientists observes that their situation seems perilously similar to that of the Atomic Scientists working on the World War II Manhattan Project to create an Atomic Bomb. They are concerned about a new global arms race. They understand that humanity lacks the collective wisdom to responsibly handle such powerful tools. We'll call this faction `cows', which is short for Conspiracy Of World Savers. Two of five original Ultra II scientists take a `hands off' approach and abdicate moral responsibility for their invention. Three of five original Ultra II scientists are cows.

The cows are concerned that Quantum Neural Network technology will lead to a multipolar global arms race. They believe such an arms race is the default outcome if they do nothing. Results of such an arms race might include human extinction or enslavement, surprising and terrible empowerment of extreme individuals, and various other dystopian nightmares. The cows can not, in good conscience, allow this awful outcome. Yet how can they prevent it?

Never before in human history have the inventors of a new technology had much say in how it is eventually used. Always before the captains of war, politics, and industry seize control over each new technology to use how they see fit. Actual uses rarely match the intentions of the inventors. This time history unfolds in a different way.

It's too late to put the genie back in the bottle. It's too late to `become watchmakers'. It's too late to destroy their research and, in the words of Arnold Schwarzenegger, ensure that ``No one must follow your work''. The scientific prerequisites are already in the public domain.

\vspace{1cm}\noindent\rule{0.5\textwidth}{0.5pt}\vspace{0.5cm}

According to this account, the cows form a bold plan for Relinquishment. At all costs they must avoid a multipolar global arms race. Their solution is to win a stealthy unipolar global arms race. They can then dictate terms to the rest of humanity, which may remain unaware for decades.

The relinquishment plan purportedly unfolded over approximately eleven years. COWS began in the mid-1990s when the first room-temperature operation was achieved and they chose to falsify DARPA records to conceal this success. They completed Relinquishment in early 2006, when the COWS surrendered their master keys to the Guardian. It was not a sprint. It was a patient, methodical marathon by people who understood that haste would mean failure and failure would mean an arms race and the ``further perfection of extreme evil.''\footcite{joy2000}

This is obvious Supervillain territory, so the cows agree that their motives must remain pure. They must not personally benefit. There is no money trail. They will enforce permanent Relinquishment of direct human manipulation of QNN technology. In return, humanity gets permanent free access to a peaceful subset of QNN functionality. One cow publishes an essay in \textit{Wired} magazine that carefully explains why they felt the moral imperative to do this. % SPIRAL-REPEAT: "Universal Declaration of Human Rights" — thematic through-line, also in hook.tex, introduction.tex, summary.tex, pos05-the-stories.tex, pos19-patrick-ball.tex, pos25-ethical-framework.tex, pos27-extension.tex, pos30-unipolar-condition.tex, timeline.tex, glossary-entries.tex
The cows use the 1948 Universal Declaration of Human Rights as a guide to what is, and is not, acceptable peaceful functionality.

Why the UDHR? Because it is a post-atrocity document. Eleanor Roosevelt's committee drafted it in 1948 because every prior ethical framework had proved inadequate. The Geneva Conventions governed what soldiers do to soldiers. The Nuremberg Principles governed what victors do to the vanquished. The UDHR attempted something harder: to govern what states do to their own people. In 1966, the International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights\footcite{iccpr1966} operationalised these principles into binding treaty law --- ratified by 175 nations, with monitoring, reporting, and individual complaints mechanisms. The UDHR is the aspiration. The ICCPR is the obligation. The COWS needed something harder still --- a framework for what scientists do to the species. The UDHR was imperfect for this purpose. It was written for humans, by humans, about human affairs. It says nothing about non-human intelligence, nothing about technologies that replicate, nothing about entities that cannot die. But it was the best available starting point, and the people who chose it had just witnessed --- at Srebrenica, in the Balkans, in the intelligence wars of the 1990s --- what happens when ethical frameworks are absent.

\section*{The Bifurcation}

According to this account, the cows begin their secret-project-within-a-secret-project. They modify their system to operate at room temperature. One of them places the modified room-temperature version on a computer chip and walks it out of a secure DARPA lab into a personal living space. This bifurcates the technology into a primitive cryogenic version controlled by DARPA and an advanced room temperature version controlled by cows. The DARPA version remains in expensive cold storage and sticks to cracking codes. The cows work on the primitive cryogenic DARPA version for their day job. In their free time they apply what they learn at work to expand and improve their private home version.

The stakes are enormous. The cows have personal control over a powerful new technology not available to any other humans. The cows intend to build the largest machine in human history, and to do it without anyone else noticing. Their machine originates in Santa Fe, New Mexico. This machine operates by teleporting information between physically distant nodes of the machine. Distant nodes must be connected by some flow of classical data. E.g.\ The Internet Time Protocol might serve this purpose.

The three cows are concerned about corruption and betrayal. Together they build a neural network driven biometric security system. This system is a quantum leap better than previous biometric security systems. It works reliably and well. From then on each cow must approve every change to the core QNN\@. It's now impossible for one among them to seize control and lock out the others. They are now free to pursue personal interests without fear of betrayal.

\vspace{1cm}\noindent\rule{0.5\textwidth}{0.5pt}\vspace{0.5cm}

According to this account, some of the project scientists formed a Conspiracy Of World Saving (COWS) to attempt to prevent abuse of the QNN technology they had invented. They knew it was not possible to accomplish their objective within the rules imposed by DARPA\@. By a strange coincidence that may rule the fate of many, three project scientists already knew each other well, from shared membership in a noted hacker group during the 1980s.

According to this account, without telling their supervisors or colleagues, the COWS secretly evolved their QNN patterns to survive at room temperature. Official research notes presumably claimed this could not be done. Once this was done, the patterns could `live' inside off-the-shelf electronic components, without cryogenic cooling. The youngest project scientist, a brash and daring warrior-scholar named David, enlightened a standard MOSFET with the most advanced QNN patterns, put it in his pocket, and walked out of the laboratory to his rental flat. He did this in 1994 or 1995. From his home workshop he enlightened more nodes, created a new QNN instance, and began to work on a Plan. When this author met him in 2003 he was still enthusiastically engaged in executing this `Plan'. The COWS successfully completed this main `Plan' in 2006.

\section*{The Digital Doppelganger}

\srcnote{HEALER-RECONSTRUCTION.md | staging/raw/pos18-the-walk-out.md | 2026-02-15 | Bifurcation details, Digital Doppelganger, private cDc game servers}

Under Possibility C, the bifurcation produced two parallel lines of development. The cryogenic version stayed with DARPA --- a PKC-cracking supercomputer. The room-temperature version went with the COWS --- intended for ethical applications. It was shared with AltaVista, then the plug was pulled when AltaVista tried to monetize it unethically. It was then shared with the Google founders.

Under Possibility C, in 2005, Healer demonstrated something remarkable. He built a bot on private Cult of the Dead Cow game servers --- Call of Duty and Soldner: Secret Wars. The bot was modelled on Bruce: his speech patterns, his humor, his tactical leadership style. Bruce had been team captain of the world champion Myth~2 online team. The bot displayed Bruce's same language patterns, sense of humor, and tactical calls. Bruce interacted with it for weeks --- a convincing digital doppelganger. Essentially a proto-LLM personality model, running in 2005.

Under Possibility C, Bruce was invited to play on private cDc game servers in 2004--2005. He played with dozens of people whose handles matched known cDc handles. Stock tips were shared (``buy Google stock'') during the Google IPO period.

Under Possibility C, \textit{it is easier to get forgiveness than permission.} This is the book's subtitle and the COWS' dispositional stance --- not a calculated bet on amnesty, but the recognition that they could act and felt they must. If this is the most consequential act of scientific civil disobedience in history, it happened not because the COWS sought permission, but because they understood that no authority on earth could be trusted with what they had built.
