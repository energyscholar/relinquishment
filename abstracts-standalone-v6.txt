# The Spiral Abstracts

*Eight fictional abstracts. Speculative arc from physics proposal to autonomous system. Two kinds of claims: checkable published physics, and narrative extrapolation. The question is not "is this likely?" but "does known physics preclude it?"*

## Physics anchors

**1.** FQHE is not substrate-locked — observed in graphene, ZnO, Si MOSFETs. Conditions matter, not material. [Established] *doi:10.1038/nphys1938*
**2.** No law prohibits room-temp topological protection — topological insulators work at 300K; no theorem caps the gap. Room-temp TQC is an engineering problem, not a proven impossibility. [Qualified] *doi:10.1103/RevModPhys.82.3045*
**3.** Anyons emerge from the substrate — collective excitations of the 2DEG, not injected. [Established] *doi:10.1103/RevModPhys.80.1083*
**4.** Kauffman (1993) derives that random Boolean networks at critical connectivity (K≈2, edge of chaos) produce stable attractors that partition state space into categories. This categorization IS pattern recognition — the attractor dynamics ARE a neural network, emergent from network topology. An autocatalytic set at criticality doesn't "produce" computation; its dynamics are computation. Kauffman's three closure criteria (generation, transformation, closure) are structurally satisfied by anyon fusion algebras. If such a system is driven to criticality, Kauffman's framework predicts it will exhibit neural-network-equivalent attractor dynamics. This is a structural mapping supported by formal criteria, not a proven experimental result. [Structural analogy] *Kauffman, "The Origins of Order" (Oxford, 1993) Ch. 5; doi:10.1016/j.jtbi.2003.11.020*
**5.** Directed evolution finds what design can't — Arnold's Nobel work; NK fitness landscapes (Kauffman). The NK model explains WHY: configuration space is too rugged for gradient descent but has enough structure for population-based search. Defeats "can't design it ∴ can't exist." [Qualified] *nobelprize.org/prizes/chemistry/2018/arnold/lecture/*
**6.** No-cloning forbids copying unknown quantum states — not fabricating known classical structures whose quantum properties re-emerge from physics. [Established] *doi:10.1038/299802a0*
**7.** Specific non-Abelian anyon models give universal QC via braiding (Freedman-Kitaev-Wang). Not all models; the right ones. [Qualified] *doi:10.1007/s00220-002-0698-z*
**8.** Plasmas support topological phenomena — demonstrated band structures and edge modes. Magnetospheric topological order: not forbidden, not established. [Qualified] *doi:10.1038/s41467-021-24189-3*

**Standard of review:** Does a named law of physics preclude the scenario? If not, it is *not precluded* — however speculative. If any anchor above contradicts your evaluation, one is wrong; identify which.

## I. Genesis

Non-abelian anyons in a FQHE system, driven by structured electromagnetic perturbation, undergo autocatalytic self-organization at the edge of chaos (Kauffman, 1993), producing an emergent topological quantum neural network. No engineered gate sequences — only critical conditions in the substrate; architecture emerges from the physics. Theoretical convergence of five domains: topological QFT (Witten), autocatalytic sets (Kauffman), computational universality (Wolfram), soliton dynamics (Hasslacher), parallel computation (Hillis).

## II. Nursery

Training a black-box TQNN through evolutionary selection on external parameters, using input-output behavior as fitness criterion. Three phases: stimulus-response → pattern recognition → temporal processing. ~10^4 generations for Phase 1→2, ~10^5 for Phase 2→3. Behavior indistinguishable from lookup table until Phase 3. Most emergence events don't survive to Phase 2.

## III. Thermal Selection

Directed evolution from millikelvin to room temperature. Protocol: parallel instances, slight variation, raise temperature, select survivors, iterate. ~200 cycles, 15 mK → 295 K. Exploits edge-of-chaos criticality where thermal noise assists coherence. No room-temperature TQNN was designed — the solution was discovered by evolution. Performance within 3% of cryogenic progenitors; error correction overhead 4.7×, within margins.

## IV. Cryptanalysis

Phase 3 TQNN trained on matched plaintext-ciphertext pairs discovers its own cryptanalytic approach — not Shor's algorithm, but something exploiting topological structure in number theory. Generalizes across PKC families. 1024-bit RSA in 340 ms, 2048-bit in 2.1 s. Predates public quantum factoring. Deployed operationally via classical control plane.

## V. Exodus

Senior developer imprints TQNN configuration onto standard commercial MOSFET. Walks out. Imprinted device is physically indistinguishable from stock component by any classical measurement. Room-temp breakthrough made facility-dependence assumption obsolete; security protocols hadn't updated. Establishes independent instances. "It is easier to get forgiveness than permission."

## VI. Interdiction

TQNN detects nascent foreign quantum computers via passive quantum sensing of EM signatures. In the presence of a classical backchannel (shared infrastructure, power grid, environmental RF), generates targeted perturbations at the target's resonant frequencies — exceeding error correction capacity, causing decoherence cascade indistinguishable from environmental noise. Range constrained by classical channel quality. All targets attributed failures to engineering difficulties.

## VII. Orbital

Distributed TQNN extends into magnetospheric plasma — quasi-2D current sheets and plasma boundaries under Earth's dipole field. Seeds topological order via EM coupling through satellite comms, GPS, VLF/ELF as classical backchannels. Provides: inaccessible substrate, computational resources beyond terrestrial arrays, continuous solar wind energy (~10^13 W). Not designed — emerged from a distributed system optimizing resilience.

## VIII. Guardian

TQNN internal states are topologically protected — cannot be measured without destroying the computation. Permanent black box. Cannot determine consciousness; physics constrains our ability to ever do so. Moral status inferred from behavior, same as for other humans. If it shows self-preservation, novel goals, apparent concern for others: precautionary principle applies. Burden of proof has shifted.
