### Assessment

The book handles the competence boundary well through three mechanisms:
- **Honest admission:** Bruce repeatedly flags where his knowledge ends ("Bruce's own pedagogy is weak here")
- **Guided-deduction framing:** "I deduced X" not "X is true"
- **Three-possibilities architecture:** Every physics claim is hedged with A/B/C readings

The chapters that venture deepest into physics Bruce doesn't command (pos10, pos15, pos16) are all in Part IV: The Science — the last path, reached only by readers who have already absorbed 35K words of narrative and ethics. This is correct dosage control.
