Concept Combinations

Bruce's idea: pictogram combos explain compound concepts visually. The reader learns individual symbols first, then encounters combinations that carry meaning without words.

⬡ + ◈ = life in the Flat
The Flat + Emergence = T3 (life in the Flat)
⬡ + ⏚ = natural habitat
The Flat + Ground/Habitat = magnetosphere as natural 2DEG
⬡ + ◈ + ⏚ = magnetospheric life
The Flat + Emergence + Habitat = magnetospheric primitive (taxonomy type 1)
◈ + ⎈ = Custodian
Emergence + Stewardship = engineered/grown life with ethical governance (taxonomy type 2)
⬡ + ⚠ = why relinquish
The Flat + Danger = the relinquishment question
