## Design Philosophy

The SVGs ARE the narrative. Text is connective tissue and emotional resonance (~600 words total). Full-viewport animations teach everything that matters. Show don't tell.

## Reference Style

Study `~/software/has-anyone-looked/tutorials/solar-wind-coupling.html` — its layout (1100px content, full-width SVGs at 900px viewBox, JS-driven interactivity, dark theme with CSS variables, play/pause controls). Match that production quality. SVGs should be SCENES — rich, detailed, cinematic. Not diagrams.

## Structure

### THE OPENING (one full-viewport SVG, ~45 seconds, auto-plays once then holds)

Start at solar system scale — Sun, planetary orbits as dots, solar wind streaming outward as visible particles. Animated viewBox (or JS-driven zoom) pushes in toward Earth-Moon system. The magnetosphere tail extends past the Moon's orbit — show this at scale. Continue zooming to standard magnetosphere cross-section: bow shock, magnetopause, plasma sheet, tail.

Throughout: a DISTANCE INDICATOR (updating scale bar) and TIME RATE INDICATOR ("1 sec = 6 hours" → "1 sec = 10 min") always visible, their changes legible and smooth.

End state: gentle breathing animation of solar wind drumming against the magnetopause. Include repeat button.

This single SVG replaces ~500 words of explanation. The viewer SEES the scale, the location, the dynamics.

### THE GATE (full-width SVG, paused, play button)

Reconnection close-up. Dayside magnetopause. X-topology forming. Energy release visible. 2-3 sentences of nature-documentary text only.

### THE CIRCUIT (full-width SVG, auto-play gentle)

Birkeland currents as animated flowing paths. Show the complete circuit: down field lines, across ionosphere, back up. Perspective view, not flat schematic. 2-3 sentences.

### THE BREATH (full-viewport SVG, ~30s, auto-plays once then holds)

Substorm cycle at whole-magnetosphere scale. Growth phase (tail stretching, energy loading), expansion (reconnection, dipolarization front racing earthward, aurora flaring), recovery. Space weather indicator visible (Kp-like, simple). Repeat button. 2-3 sentences.

### CLOSING

One quiet sentence over the final frame: "This is happening right now, above your head, whether anyone is watching or not."
