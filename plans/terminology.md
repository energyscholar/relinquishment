## Core Argument (~40 words, no Guardian)

Five independent lines of research converged in the early 1990s to make a new kind of quantum computer theoretically possible. If anyone built it, the resulting technology would be too powerful for any single entity to control — and the only responsible action would be to give it up permanently. This book presents evidence that someone may have done exactly that, and asks what you would do.
