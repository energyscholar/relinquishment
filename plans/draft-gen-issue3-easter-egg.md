The intent: the deeper material doesn't disappear. It moves from the main argument to a layer the curious reader chooses to enter. The main text stays clean — institutional plausibility, named reconstruction, your Sagan register. The easter eggs reward the reader who wants to go further.

So as you work on ULTRA II positioning: the introduction you're drafting is the right scope and register for what stays visible. Most of the rest will be hidden and findable.
