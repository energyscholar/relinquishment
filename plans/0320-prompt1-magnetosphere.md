## Auditor Corrections (post-generation)

Two corrections applied by Auditor after Generator completed the artifact:

1. **Physics accuracy:** "a hundred thousand large power stations" → "several thousand large power stations." Solar wind delivers ~5 TW to magnetopause; reconnection transfers ~0.5-2.5 TW. Generator overestimated by ~50×.

2. **Accessibility (prefers-reduced-motion):** Generator used CSS `animation-play-state:paused` which does not affect SMIL `<animate>` elements. Added JS `pauseAnimations()` call gated on `matchMedia('(prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)')` so auto-play SVGs actually stop for users requesting reduced motion.
