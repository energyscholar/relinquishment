### Arc 5: "We Already Did It, Mate" (Parts 5-6)
- Location: girlfriend's house, living room, mid-morning, 2004
- Context: Bruce assembling ramifications of QNN technology — dual-use, nuclear codes, arms race

### Arc 6: The Joy Article Discovery & CADIE (Part 6)

- Coda: why Bruce treasured the mentorship, chess drubbing, "better than MIT"
