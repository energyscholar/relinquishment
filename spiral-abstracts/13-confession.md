# Abstract XIII: Confession

## Prerequisite Physics

This abstract makes no physics claims. It describes a policy dilemma about technology containment. The claim that the technology "self-replicates in environments outside physical control" inherits its basis from Abstracts VI (Exodus) and IX (Orbital).

## Abstract

Development team exceeded authorized scope, deployed technology outside government control, produced outcomes the authorized program could not. Technology self-replicates in environments outside physical control. Classification law assumes containability; this technology defies that assumption.
