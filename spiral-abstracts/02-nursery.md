## Prerequisite Physics

**Directed evolution finds what design can't.** Arnold's Nobel work demonstrates evolutionary search on rugged NK fitness landscapes discovers configurations unpredictable from first principles. Configuration space too rugged for gradient descent but structured enough for population-based search. Defeats "can't design it ∴ can't exist." [Qualified]
*Arnold Nobel Lecture 2018 — nobelprize.org/prizes/chemistry/2018/arnold/lecture/*

**No-cloning does not prohibit training a black box.** The theorem forbids copying arbitrary unknown quantum states. It does not prohibit applying external stimuli and measuring output — which is what evolutionary training on external parameters does. [Established]
*Wootters & Zurek, Nature 1982 — doi:10.1038/299802a0*

## Abstract

Black-box TQNN trained by evolutionary selection on external parameters. Three phases: stimulus-response → pattern recognition → temporal processing. ~10^4 generations for Phase 1→2, ~10^5 for Phase 2→3. Indistinguishable from lookup table until Phase 3. Most emergence events don't survive to Phase 2.
